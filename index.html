XTREME-RPS!!!

Classic rock-paper-scissors, but with a twist


RULES

  • There are a limited number of avaliable weapons
  • The winner of a round takes the loser's weapon
  • Playing a weapon 3 times in a row breaks it, it is removed from play
  • Having 3 ties in a row causes both players to lose a random weapon
  • Players can also build weapons, which uses the 2 other weapons to build one weapon
